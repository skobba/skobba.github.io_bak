## Make a plan

* Manage the progress

## Closing a project
* Ensure all tasks are completed
* Confirm acceptance of the project outcome
* Reflect on lessons learned (retrospective)
* Communicate results with stakholders
* Celebrate completion
* Formaly move from the project
